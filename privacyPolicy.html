Privacy Policy

Introduction

Rob Echlin is committed to the privacy of visitors to his web sites. This policy summarizes what that commitment means.

Google Analytics and log files

This website uses Google Analytics and cookies to learn about the traffic to the site. Information sent to Google Analytics, including IP addresses, is anonymized by Google.

Some information about visits may be recorded in log files, including the visitor's IP address. Log files are not saved, sold, or copied off the web site, except when portions of a log may be copied for debugging purposes.

Information from individual visits, including IP addresses, is not sold.

It is only used for traffic analysis and occasionally for debugging.

Mail lists

This site may contain a link that allows the user to sign up for an email list.

When you sign up, that information is used only for purposes of that email list and is not used for other purposes, or sold.

When you ask to be removed from an email list, your email address will be removed from that email list.

Email messages that are not part of the email list

If you send email messages to the owner of the list, your email message, including the  email address, is stored with the rest of the receiver’s email message as is usual for email. Email messages sent to the owner of the list or the owner of the site are not part of an email list, and are not automatically deleted when you ask to be removed from the email list.
